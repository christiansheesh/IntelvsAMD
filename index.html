Intel vs AMD

When considering which computer is best in your work or business it is important to look at all the computer features that make it run. At the center of that decision is a CPU, or Computer Processing Unit, because that is the part that runs a program. Two giants are leading the production of CPUs in the current market. Intel and Advanced Micro Devices, or AMD as it is commonly known, are these manufacturers. That’s why we’ll briefly explains some of the two companies are best of, when it comes to pricing, performance, specs, graphics, memory sizes, and even more.
